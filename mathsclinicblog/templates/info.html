Some of cauchymike's data science and programming projects can be found here.

Enough has been said about cauchymike, what exactly is data science? According to Wikipedia, "Data science is an inter-disciplinary field that uses scientific methods, processes, algorithms and systems to extract knowledge and insights from many structural and unstructured data." I love to define data science as the use of data to solve problems. With that definition, we can argue that everyone is a data scientist.

Let me give a very simple example to butress my definition, suppose you want to go to work tommorow, you must have enough data to make some decisions like, waking up by 5 A.M, getting to the bus stop before 7 A.M, eating or deciding not to eat, and so on. This decision making, based on some data you have due to experience or data from random calculative guess is the process of data science.
