Some of cauchymike's data science and programming projects can be found here.

Enough has been said about cauchymike, what exactly is data science? According to Wikipedia, "Data science is an inter-disciplinary field that uses scientific methods, processes, algorithms and systems to extract knowledge and insights from many structural and unstructured data." I love to define data science as the use of data to solve problems. With that definition, we can argue that everyone is a data scientist.

Let me give a very simple example to butress my definition, suppose you want to go to work tommorow, you must have enough data to make some decisions like, waking up by 5 A.M, getting to the bus stop before 7 A.M, eating or deciding not to eat, and so on. This decision making, based on some data you have due to experience or data from random calculative guess is the process of data science.

The Goals and Objectives of Data Science Made Easy blog!

I will highlight some of the goals and objectives of this website. But I will quickly point out that this website is only part of a bigger plan. Through the use of the blogpost section of this website, registered users can write blog posts on any concept or topic related to maths and science. In particular, Cauchymike will be writing bloposts on Python programming language and Data science. There will be atleast one blog post every week. At the end of every blogpost, it is advised that blogpost users include their contact email address at the end of each blogpost.

The goals of this website are listed below;

  • Increase learning rate through the "learn by teaching others" technique. This technique is one of the most efficient way of learning a new skill
  • Young individuals and beginners in a particular discipline get the opportunity to develop their confidence. They also get to test their understanding of the concept they are writing about.
  • Users can always use the blogposts as a reference to their peers to show a level of competency. After a while, when those interested in data science have developed a level of understanding of data science, our achievements will be posted here.
  • For the users in Basic school(Primary school), this website may be too advanced for their consumption. But there will be good materials they can download and read. Its also a good idea if they(Users in basic school and Some in Junior secondary schhol) try to glance through blogposts containig relevant educational information.
  • Finally, this website will contain information regarding relevant articles on educational oportunities and news on advancement of technology. There will also be a WHATSAPP GROUP created specifically to discuss issues related to maths and science. Most especially data science
